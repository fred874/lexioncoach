What LEXION does

Built for the actual IELTS exam

Not generic English practice. Every exercise maps to a real IELTS question type, scored the way the real exam is.

✍️
Writing — Task 1 & Task 2
Submit an essay and get instant AI feedback covering task achievement, coherence, lexical resource, and grammar. Handwritten? Use the camera to scan it in.
📖
Reading Practice
True/False/NG, matching headings, sentence completion, summary cloze — the full question set, with explanations when you get one wrong.
🎧
Listening Drills
Audio tracks for all four sections. Multiple choice, map labelling, sentence completion. Score is shown the moment you finish.
🎙️
Speaking — Parts 1, 2 & 3
Record a 60-second response to a real cue card. Review it, re-record it, and build the habit of speaking on demand.
📚
Vocabulary Builder
IELTS-specific words, definitions, phonetics, and example sentences. Test yourself with daily quiz mode. A new word every day.
📊
Band Score Tracker
Your estimated band score updates after every session. You can see exactly how far you are from your goal and which skill is dragging you down.
