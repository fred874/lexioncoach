Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Zero personal data collected

This Privacy Policy describes how Lexion IELTS Coach ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the LEXION mobile application ("App"). We have designed LEXION with your privacy as a first principle.

Information We Do Not Collect

LEXION does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. All study data — including essays, speaking recordings, vocabulary progress, and band scores — is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.

Information Processed Temporarily

When you use the AI writing evaluation feature (Pro users only), your essay text is sent to our secure server proxy solely to obtain feedback from an AI language model. This text is:

  • Not stored or logged on our servers
  • Not used for any purpose other than generating your feedback
  • Transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection
  • Never shared with third parties

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment data.

💳  We never see your credit card details. All billing is handled directly by Apple.

Notifications

If you grant notification permissions, LEXION sends local study reminders. These are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any data transmission to us or any third party.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites. There are no advertising identifiers used in LEXION.

🚫  No Google Analytics. No Firebase. No Facebook SDK. No advertising networks. Nothing.

Children's Privacy

LEXION is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users at least 30 days before any material changes take effect by posting a notice in the App.
